Reports

County Contact Listing
  • The County Contact Listing report will display phone numbers and web site information for each county.
  • To generate the County Contact Listing report, click "Reports -> County Contact Listing"
  • Select "All Counties" from the left side table.
  • The reports will generate and be visable in the window.
  • You can zoom in and out with the magnify buttons -
  • If the report is multiple pages, you can click the "Previous" or "Next"
    • so cycle through them.
  • Click the "Print Report" button to print.
  • To adjust the setup of the page, click the "Page Setup" button
Contest Vote Summary
  • The Contest Vote Summary report gives a detailed view of the election. It will display the number and percentage of votes for each candidate, the number and percentage of reporting units, and the total number of votes for each county and contest.
  • To generate the Contest Vote Summary report, click "Reports -> Contest Vote Summary"
  • Select an election date from the left side table.
  • You can select active, or inactive elections using the dropdown box in the lower left hand corner of the window.
  • The reports will generate and be visable in the window.
  • You can zoom in and out with the magnify buttons -
  • If the report is multiple pages, you can click the "Previous" or "Next"
    • so cycle through them.
  • Click the "Print Report" button to print.
  • To adjust the setup of the page, click the "Page Setup" button
